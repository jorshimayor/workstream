# Final Product Strategy Review

Reviewer role: Product Strategy Reviewer.

Scope: markdown planning package.

## Findings

### High: First value must stay operational, not marketplace-oriented

Finding:

The plan correctly positions Workstream as a control plane. The biggest product risk is drifting into marketplace, agent workspace, or blockchain settlement before the workflow proves itself.

Suggested change:

Keep v0.1 limited to manual task intake, project guides, queues, checkers,
review, revision, contribution and compensation fulfillment records, and
reputation events.

Status: addressed in `docs/product_brief.md`, `docs/roadmap_30_day_master_plan.md`, and decision records.

### High: Day-7 thin slice is necessary

Finding:

A 30-day plan can become too broad unless the first usable workflow is forced early.

Suggested change:

Add a day-7 thin slice: project, guide, task, screening, ready, claim, submit, checker result, review decision.

Status: fixed in `docs/roadmap_30_day_master_plan.md`.

### Medium: The first operator value is now clear

Finding:

The product should be judged by fewer missed rules, fewer avoidable revisions,
faster review readiness, and cleaner compensation fulfillment tracking.

Suggested change:

Keep these as pilot success checks, not marketing copy.

Status: addressed in `docs/product_brief.md`.
